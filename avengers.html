Genius billionaire inventor in a high-tech suit.

The mighty Norse of Thunder wielding Mjolnir.

Hulk (Bruce Banner)

Scientist turned green behemoth with immense strength.

Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff)

Master spy and martial artist with a dark past.

Hawkeye (Clint Barton)

Master archer and skilled marksman.

Reality-warping mutant with chaos magic.

Spider-Man (Peter Parker)

Teenage web-slinger with superpowers from a radioactive spider bite.

Doctor Strange (Stephen Strange)

Master of the mystic arts and sorcerer supreme.

Captain Marvel (Carol Danvers)

Cosmic-powered heroine with incredible strength and energy manipulation.
