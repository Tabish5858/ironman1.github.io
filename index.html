What I have Done So Far!

IronMan

Iron Man (2008): Billionaire genius Tony Stark transforms into the armored superhero Iron Man, using his ingenuity to escape captivity and protect the world as a technologically advanced vigilante.

IronMan2

Iron Man 2 (2010): As Tony Stark faces personal demons and government pressure, he unveils new suits and battles villains, including Whiplash, to maintain his dual identity as Iron Man.

IronMan3

Iron Man 3 (2013): Struggling with anxiety post-Avengers, Tony Stark confronts the ruthless Mandarin and discovers the true power lies within himself, making him question the man behind the armor.

Avengers:
Assemble

The Avengers (2012): Tony Stark/Iron Man joins forces with Earth's mightiest heroes to battle Loki and his alien army, proving his value as a brilliant strategist and indispensable asset to the Avengers.

Avenger:
Age of Ultron

Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015): Tony Stark's creation of Ultron, an AI peacekeeping program, backfires, leading to a cataclysmic battle as he grapples with the consequences of his actions and the importance of unity within the Avengers team.

Avenger:
Infinity War

Avengers: Infinity War (2018): Iron Man plays a pivotal role in the fight against the powerful cosmic warlord Thanos, demonstrating his determination to protect the universe and facing heartbreaking sacrifices in the battle to prevent the Mad Titan from collecting all the Infinity Stones.

Avenger:
End Game

Avengers: Infinity War (2018): Tony Stark, as Iron Man, leads the Avengers in a desperate battle against Thanos, a ruthless warlord, as he tries to gather the all-powerful Infinity Stones to unleash his devastating plans upon the universe.

IronMan Vs Mad Hulk

Iron Man's battles with the Hulk were epic clashes of raw power versus advanced technology, as Tony Stark's armored suit was put to the test against the uncontrollable fury of the Hulk's immense strength.

IronMan Vs Captain America

In confrontations with Captain America, Iron Man engaged in intense battles, pitting his technologically advanced suit against the Super Soldier's incredible combat skills and unyielding determination.

IronMan Vs Thanos

Facing the formidable threat of Thanos, Iron Man displayed unwavering resolve and tactical brilliance, utilizing every resource at his disposal to take on the Mad Titan and protect the universe.
